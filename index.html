About redCall

redCall allows you to share moments by making a phone call to many people at once. You will hear from your friends, community and celebrities live and more personally than ever!

The way it works: your audience add you as a speaker on the redCall app, after which whenever you want to share with them a moment, you would go on redCall, record a voice message and press send. Instantaneously, your audience phones will ring as if you are calling them - exactly replicating a phone call. Your audience would pick up and listen to your call.
